About us

Our platform offers a comprehensive comparison tool, allowing you to easily evaluate specifications, performance, and prices across multiple brands. Whether you're a gamer, professional, or student. TechGauge provides detailed reviews, expert recommendations, and the latest deals to ensure you get the best tech for your needs.

Our Mission

At TechGauge, our mission is to provide accurate and reliable information to help users make informed decisions when choosing the right laptop. Our goal is to inspire smart technology choices by offering personalized recommendations and expert insights into the latest tech trends. We are committed to saving users time and money by delivering the best deals, up-to-date pricing, and product reviews, ensuring they make efficient and budget-friendly decisions.

Our Story

In August 2024, as students in Institut Teknologi Sumatera, we were given an assignment during a student association event where we were tasked with creating a technology-themed website. The project challenged us to think critically about what kind of website could truly benefit users in today's tech-driven world. From that assignment, we developed the idea of creating a website that allows users to compare laptops based on their specifications and budget. This initial assignment sparked our interest and became the foundation for TechGauge.
